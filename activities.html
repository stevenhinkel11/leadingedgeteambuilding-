Apollo Simulation

3-4 hours 8-100

Apollo 13 mission simulation, with teams as astronauts and mission control solving creative challenges.

Apollo Simulation

3-4 hours 8-100

Oxygen is running out. Power is running out. Time is running out. Failure is not an option: the astronauts must make it back to earth.

This simulation brings to life aspects of the Apollo 13 mission, and draws parallels to the creative problem solving, teamwork, and communication that allowed the astronauts to survive against all odds.

Your team is divided into two groups: astronauts, and mission control. Each group is facing its own challenges while simultaneously attempting to solve the overarching catastrophic issues that must be resolved in order for the astronauts to return safely.

Team Chronicle

2-3 hours 8-50

Team Chronicle is the perfect activity for groups who want to reflect on their journey, align around their vision, and create something lasting together. This program blends storytelling, creativity, and collaboration in a highly engaging way.

Teams take on different roles—writers, illustrators, interviewers, and more—as they co-create a storybook that captures the essence of their organization. With limited resources and the need for crisp alignment, participants must communicate clearly, think strategically, and support one another. The result is a tangible artifact—part vision, part narrative—that inspires the team long after the activity ends.

Blueprint Simulation

3-4.5 hours 15-40

The task: build the machine. Your customer is counting on it. The only problem is, your team has never built this type of machine before and you have very limited and scattered information about how to do it. Oh, and there may be a few other problems too. Tough customer, change orders, resource constraints, and limited ability to communicate with other parts of the team pervade this simulation.

It's a bit of a "fun meets serious" situation. We're pretty sure you'll walk away with some new insights about effective teamwork, as well as a little contraption that you never thought you'd need.
